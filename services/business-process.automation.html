Business Process Automation

Business Process Automation (BPA) refers to the application of technology to automate relevant business practices…a solution that only elite professionals can make work for you.

At TORF Technologies, we believe that BPA is more than process automation; it is an expert balance of people, process and technology designed to exceed your business goals.

With us, you will enjoy:
  • Expert Analysis: Through due diligence, honesty and application of decades of experience, we will help you determine which business processes need to be automated, and the best technologies to use. Many business waste money automating the wrong processes or automating the right ones but in the wrong way. With TORF Technologies, you run no such risk.
  • A Wholesome Solution: Whatever your automation goals, we always make sure that the big three business objectives are achieved, namely:
    • Improvement of service quality
    • Improvement of service delivery
    • Cost-saving
  • Professionalism: Don’t just have a BPA service delivered to you.
    • Define clear automation goals.
    • Have it evaluated with international standard measurement of Return on Investment. Ensure that everyone concerned in the business processes is involved to determine the best solutions.
    • Train employees to use the solution.
    • Have a backup plan where automation fails, to avoid serious disruption.
    • And, ensure that there is continuous evaluation and improvement of the BPA solution long after deployment
  • Full Automation: Automate every relevant business process with TORF Technologies, such as:
    • Employee Collaboration including document management
    • Stakeholders Collaboration
    • Examination and Marking Systems
    • HR processes including On-boarding and Payroll
    • Customer Service and Employee Self-service
    • Tracking and Alerts
    • Manufacturing processes
    • Inventory
    • Other repetitive tasks in your organization
